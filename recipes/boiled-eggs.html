Hard Boiled Eggs

Image credits: allrecipes.com
This is the perfect dish for a good breakfast, it's nutritious, fulfilling, and delicious, it literally cannot get any better! Not only that, it's also cheap, and it's easy to make, I have this for breakfast almost daily. These eggs are cooked to perfection, all the people like me who do not like their eggs runny, gather here because this is the one and only right way to make boiled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 10 Eggs
  • Tap water
  • Salt
  • Vinegar (optinal)

Steps

  1. Fill a medium sized pot with water
  2. Add vinegar and salt to the water (be generous with the salt, use a few table spoons)
  3. Put the pot on high heat and wait for the water to boil
  4. After the water has boiled, reduce heat and put the eggs inside the pot
  5. Cover the pot and let the eggs boil for 15 minutes
  6. Take out the eggs and put them in ice water for 5 minutes to stop their cooking
  7. peel the eggs and enjoy your meal
