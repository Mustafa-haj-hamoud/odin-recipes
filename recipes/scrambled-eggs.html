Perfect Scrambled Eggs


image credits: allrecipes.com

Do you like your scrambled eggs cooked all the way? nothing runny or creamy. If so, then this recipe is for you. It might be a hot take, but I do not enjoy runny eggs in any form. Most scrambled eggs recipes out there do not cook the eggs all the way through, intentionally, which blows my mind. However, there's none of that here, no liquid in these eggs :D

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • Vegetable oil (1 table spoon)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Veggies (optional)

Steps

  1. In a pan, put a table spoon of oil and heat it up
  2. If you want to add veggies to your eggs, now is the time to saute them
  3. Break the eggs and mix them up in a bowl until they have a homogeneous consistency, do not forget to add salt
  4. After the oil is hot, reduce heat and add the eggs to the pan
  5. Stir the eggs with a wooden spoon until fully cooked
  6. after the eggs are cooked, put some pepper on top and enjoy your eggs with a piece of bread
